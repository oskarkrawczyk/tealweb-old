Manufacturing Defect Artistic Series by Zulu Kuki

  • Client: Levi's
  • Scope: 360°

The assumption of the project was to cooperate with various artists whose works appeared as works of art on Levi's Trucker Jackets and then to present them during a unique event in a traditional art gallery. Each of the works has been laid out in an incorrect way, for example, skewed and shifted. On top of that, every jacket had a stamp informing that this is a production defect.

After the event, the exhibition was moved to the urban space for a month to present it to a wider circle of people.

Every jacket had a stamp informing that this is a production defect

Project details

  • Cooperation with 13 artists from various fields of art (Rosalie., Sandra Kpodonou, Barrakuz, Beeski, Jorge Alva, Łukasz Ziętek, Maksym Rudnik, Mr. Osom, Marta Kudu, NAWER, Riamone, Swanski, Holak).

  • Event organization and exhibition in the urban space.
  • Support for communication with the media.

  • Creative concept

Photo shoot

Photo session presenting Levi’s tracker jackets created as part of the Manufacturing Defect Artistic Series project. The inspiration for the creation of photographs was the technique of analog photography, which often involves various defects. The photos were created in a scenery reminiscent of an artistic studio.
