Manufacturing Defect Artistic Series - ARTWORKS

  • Client − Levi's
  • Scope − Production

The assumption of the Manufacturing Defect Artistic Series by Zulu Kuki project was to cooperate with various artists whose works appeared as works of art on Levi's Trucker Jackets and then to present them during a unique event in a traditional art gallery.

We cooperated with 13 artists from various fields of art: Rosalie. (music), Sandra Kpodonou (fashion), Barrakuz (collage & graphic design), Beeski (illustration & street art.), Jorge Alva (photography), Łukasz Ziętek (photography), Maksym Rudnik (photography), Mr. Osom (graphic design), Marta Kudu (tattoo art), NAWER (design & street art.), Riamone (street art.), Swanski (illustration & street art.), Holak (music & graphic design). As a result, 11 unique works were created.
