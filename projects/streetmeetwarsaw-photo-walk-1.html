#STREETMEETWARSAW Photowalk

  • Client − Puma
  • Scope − Production & PR

The main goal was to organize creative photo-walks for people who are interested in urban photography. The ambassadors were 6 Polish photographers working with various techniques (reportage, fashion photography, commercial photography) - it allowed us to expand the reception group and reach out to diverse, creative people.

Our actions:

  • cooperation with 6 photo ambassadors
  • creating photo content with every ambassador and graphic content for communication
  • organization of a photo-walk - creating an attractive route around the city, logistic facilities, merch for participants, evening dinner
  • PR support - communication with the media
