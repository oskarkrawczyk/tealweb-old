#STREETMEETWARSAW Photowalk

  • Client: Puma
  • Scope: Production & PR

The main goal was to organize creative photowalks for people who are interested in urban photography. The ambassadors were six photographers from Poland, working with various techniques (reportage, fashion photography, commercial photography) - this allowed us to expand the target group and reach out to more diverse, and creative people.

The ambassadors were six photographers from Poland

Project details

  • Cooperation with 6 photo ambassadors.
  • Creating photo content with every ambassador and graphic content for communication.
  • Organization of a photo-walk - creating an attractive route around the city, logistic facilities, merch for participants, evening dinner.
  • PR support - communication with the media.
