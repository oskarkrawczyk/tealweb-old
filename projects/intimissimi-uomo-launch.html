Intimissimi Uomo − Launch of the brand in Poland

  • Client: Intimissimi
  • Scope: Event

Intimissimi Uomo is the version of the Intimissimi brand dedicated for men.

  We were responsible for the presentation of the brand during its premiere in Poland. The event was organized for journalists and clients.

  To reflect the brand's DNA and character, we've built a man cave in the form of a garage with a classic car in the shopping mall (Galeria Mokotów in Warsaw).

  In the workshop space, guests could personalize products by selecting their favorite designs or fonts. For automotive fans, we presented a classic muscle car (Ford Mustang).
