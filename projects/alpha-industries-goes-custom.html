ALPHA INDUSTRIES Goes Custom

  • Client: Alpha Industries
  • Scope: PR

Image-artistic activities allowing to build the image of the Alpha Industries brand on the Polish market.

We wanted to present brand and its history to wider target group by working with carefully selected people from the world of music, art and photography.

Our tool to achieve that was customization - very hot trend right now that is gaining popularity every day.

With individual approach to that issue, we’ve linked together ambassadors that have highly defined aesthetics with artist who would perfectly portray ambassadors’ personality and style.

5 unique projects were created, which were then communicated in the media.

5 unique projects were created, for carefully selected names from the world of music, art and photography.

Project details

  • Cooperation with 5 artists from various fields of art (graphic design, illustration, poster, tattoos, collage) - Aneta Klejnowska, Patryk Hardziej, Krystian Ścigalski (Why Duck), Nikodem Pręgowski, Łukasz Żera (Luk21) who’s prepared personalized projects for Alpha Industries jackets for selected influencers.
  • Jackets customization - coordination of production.
  • Social media communication on artists and influencers channels.
