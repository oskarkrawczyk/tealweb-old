Illustration Day

  • Client − Reserved
  • Scope − Production

Artistic cooperation of the brand with illustrator Renata Krawczyk. Live-art in which observers decided on the final shape of the illustration. The inspiration was branching narration, especially popular in computer games or recently TV shows. The first such activation on Polish Instagram.

Results:

  • first activation of this type on Polish Instagram
  • commitment - 45,000 people (max commitment within the brand channel)
  • total range of social media channels: 795,000 recipients
  • votes - on average 1,800 people, the best moment - over 3,300 people
