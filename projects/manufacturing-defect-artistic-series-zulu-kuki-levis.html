Manufacturing Defect Artistic Series by Zulu Kuki x Levi’s

  • Client − Levi's
  • Scope − Production

The assumption of the project was to cooperate with various artists whose works appeared as works of art on Levi's Trucker Jackets and then to present them during a unique event in a traditional art gallery.

Each of the works has been laid out in an incorrect way, for example skewed and shifted. On top of that every jacket had a stamp informing that this is a production defect.

After the event, the exhibition was moved to the urban space for a month to present it to a wider circle of people.

Activation activities:

  • cooperation with 13 artists from various fields of art (Rosalie., Sandra Kpodonou, Barrakuz, Beeski, Jorge Alva, Łukasz Ziętek, Maksym Rudnik, Mr. Osom, Marta Kudu, NAWER, Riamone, Swanski, Holak)
  • production of creative content for communication (photo & video sessions)
  • event organization and exhibition in the urban space
  • support for communication with the media
