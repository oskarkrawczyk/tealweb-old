#STREETWEARNOMADS Photo tour

  • Client − Puma, Jameson Whiskey
  • Scope − Production

#STREETWEARNOMADS - is an Instagram tag that focuses on people interested in photography and street fashion.

We gave people the chance of regular, creative meetings in reality, during events organized in various cities in Poland - Gdańsk, Kraków, and Warsaw. Events gathered over 10,000 participants.

Activities (360) as part of activating #STREETWEARNOMADS:

  • strategy and communication
  • cooperation with media & influencers
  • production of photo and video sessions for the promotion of events
  • activities in social media
  • organization of 3 events
