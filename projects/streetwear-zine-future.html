Streetwear Zine "未来"

  • Client: teal.
  • Scope: 360°

Streetwear Zine is an artistic project in which the creators combine in an unconventional way the themes of street fashion, art and everything that accidentally gets into their hands during the creation of the project. By cutting completely from past editions, the third issue touched on the subject of the future (未来).

Photo shoot & Video

Everyone sees the future a bit differently - some less, others more optimistic. The Streetwear Zine presented it in 6 different scenes, during photo sessions with one hero, Sandra Najda. We were working with one model, because we wanted to create a variety of alternative visions of the future.

All sessions combine one video material in which the creative concept is explained.

Event

The premiere of zine took place in VEGAN RAMEN SHOP. Japanese culture was the inspiration. During the day, the FUTU SHIO ramen was served, whose recipe was inspired by the future.

Zines were given away from a Japanese-style vending machine.

Zine is also an art, therefore, for artistic interpretation of the slogan: FUTURE, we invited illustrators, and graphic artists from Poland and abroad.

During the event took place a vernissage of their works: Damian Dideńko (Didiusz), Maria Regucka, Mateusz Holak, Lily Walczuk (daughter of Mr OSOM.), Serge Sergio, Bartek Stefanowicz, Janek Mazur, Violetta (Zulu Kuki's mother), Damian Denis (dou.bled).
