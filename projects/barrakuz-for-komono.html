Barrakuz for KOMONO - Signature Collection Launch

  • Client − Komono
  • Scope − Production

Polish artist's participation in a global project - the premiere of the Signature Collection in which artists from around the world were involved. Each of them was supposed to interpret KOMONO products in their own style. On the day of the premiere, there was a vernissage of works and a celebratory dinner, followed by a trip around Antwerp, the city from which the brand originated.

We were responsible for:

  • artist's recommendations and coordination of Barrakuz's cooperation with the brand
  • coordination of the artist's trip and media for the premiere of the Antwerp collection
  • activities with the media
