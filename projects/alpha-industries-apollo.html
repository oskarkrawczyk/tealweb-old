Alpha Industries − Apollo − creative mailing

  • Client: Alpha Industries
  • Scope: Production

That's one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind - Neil Armstrong. 

In honor of the 50th anniversary of the Moon Landing, we've prepared a space activation for Alpha Industries, brand that has been associated with NASA for years.

We invited the illustrator Ada Zielińska to cooperate, who created a poster inspired by the APOLLO 11 mission. This together with the T-shirt from the Alpha Industries Apollo collection was sent to influencers in a special, metal case.

  
Package containing Alpha Industries Apollo 50 Reflective T and a poster with several information about the moon landing and the next NASA Mars Rover Mission, all packed up in a silver metal box which was closed with Alpha Industries legendary Remove Before Flight tag.

Creative production

As part of the project, we also carried out photo sessions with selected influencers. The photo materials were used in social media communication.
